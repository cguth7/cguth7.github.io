DR. RICK GUTHMANN

Family Medicine Physician & Clinical Researcher

Dr. Richard A. Guthmann is a board-certified family medicine physician practicing in Chicago, Illinois. With over two decades of clinical experience, he is affiliated with Advocate Illinois Masonic Medical Center and has dedicated his career to providing comprehensive primary care while advancing medical knowledge through clinical research.

Dr. Guthmann earned his medical degree from McGaw Medical Center of Northwestern University and holds a Master of Public Health (MPH). His research focuses on hypertension management, diabetes treatment, and evidence-based clinical practices. He has authored numerous peer-reviewed publications and is committed to translating medical research into improved patient outcomes.
