Measuring the True Impact of NIH Genomic Research

GWAS Discoveries and Pharmaceutical Innovation Spillovers

Key Finding

  • GWAS discoveries don't just benefit direct targets
  • Innovation spills over to biologically related gene-disease pairs through pathway networks
  • For every directly-linked pair, 60 additional pairs see increased pharmaceutical patenting
  • 707K gene-disease pairs affected across 21 years of patent data
