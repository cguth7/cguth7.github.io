A Novel Landscape Model for Gene-Disease Relationships

Quantifying Knowledge Proximity Through Biological Networks

Pathway Distance (KEGG)

  • 1-hop: Direct pathway neighbors
  • 2-hop: Shared pathway partners
  • 3-hop: Extended network
  • 30K+ genes, avg 31 connections

Disease Hierarchy (MeSH)

  • Tree-structured taxonomy
  • Levels 3-5 optimal granularity
  • 924 diseases mapped
  • Fuzzy matching at 90%

Patent-Gene-Disease

  • 140M patent associations
  • 7M unique gene-disease pairs
  • 21 years (2000-2020)
  • 99% confidence filter

Why This Matters for NLM

This framework enables rigorous measurement of how NIH-funded discoveries propagate through the biomedical knowledge graph, providing evidence-based ROI assessment for genomic research investments.
