About

Myles Friedman is a trading analyst at Performance Trust with a strong foundation in economics and quantitative analysis. His work combines rigorous economic theory with practical financial market applications.

A 2022 graduate of Northwestern University, Myles distinguished himself through his honors thesis on tax policy and the sharing economy, demonstrating his ability to tackle complex economic questions with both analytical precision and real-world relevance.

Taxing Airbnb: A Boston Case Study

This undergraduate honors thesis examined the economic and policy implications of taxation in the sharing economy, using Boston's Airbnb market as a lens to understand broader questions of regulatory frameworks, tax incidence, and market efficiency in digital platforms.
